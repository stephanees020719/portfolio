Pokemon Blog

Imagine a vibrant world where you can explore the incredible realm of Pokémon. This React application, known as 'Pokemon Blog,' makes it all possible. By tapping into the PokeAPI, it gathers information about a wide array of Pokémon, presenting it in a user-friendly list format. Whether you're hunting for your favorite Pokémon or curious about new ones, this app simplifies the journey. Search by name, summon more Pokémon with ease, and dive into the captivating details of each creature. It's all at your fingertips, thanks to the neatly organized components like PokemonDirectory, SearchBar, Home, About, and Nav. Get ready to embark on your Pokémon adventure, powered by the magic of React Router navigation.
